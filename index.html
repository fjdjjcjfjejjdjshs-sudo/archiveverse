Reference-Grade Standards

Every title in our holdings meets the highest fidelity benchmarks

4K UHD

Native 3840x2160 resolution with HDR10+ and wide color gamut. Four times the detail of Full HD.

Blu-ray ISO & REMUX

Full disc images and lossless remuxes preserved at original bitrate. Complete menus, extras, and commentary tracks.

Dolby Vision

Dynamic metadata HDR with scene-by-scene optimization. 12-bit color depth delivering over 68 billion colors.

Dolby Atmos

Object-based immersive audio with height channels. Sound flows around and above you in a true 3D soundscape.

Up to 192kHz/24bit studio-grade audio. FLAC, DSD, and master tape recordings for audiophile-level fidelity.

NAS Media Server

Enterprise-grade NAS with PB-scale storage. Organized library with metadata, subtitles, and multi-device streaming.

Our Mission

Our team of curators meticulously selects, verifies, and maintains every item in our holdings, ensuring the highest standards of quality and completeness.
